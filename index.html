The games your child already loves to play can actually help them develop important skills, often without them even realizing it! Just like vegetables hidden in their favorite meal to make it healthy while still enjoyable, Hidden Vegetables shows you the learning that is hidden in games kids already enjoy. Click through the games below to discover the skills your child can build while having fun!
